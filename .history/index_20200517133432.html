About Me

Hi, I’m a 17 year old from a small country called Latvia and I’ve always loved taking photos. Photography, to me, is catching a moment in one frame before it’s gone forever. Every photo is a memory I wish I could re-live.

I got my first digital camera from my godfather. When I began taking photos, it was a way to get me out of my comfort zone and to truly experience every moment I was in. As I grew and got better with time, it gave me more motivation to try new, more advanced things like astrophotography. Seeing a photo of the starry sky appear on the camera’s digital screen in the middle of the night or looking back at breathtaking landscape photos from a road trip is what motivates me to get out and see the world.

In the portfolio section of this website I put some of my best pictures into 6 different groups. There you’ll find pictures that give you a little insight of my life.
